Actualize Capstone Project

For my final project at Actualize, I built an app called Bookends, which is intended to build a community around an individual activity - reading. Readers can come together to share recommendations, track reading progress and enrich their lives through the discovery of new books and new relationships with fellow readers. Bookends was created with a Rails backend and Vue.js frontend incorporating Google Books API.

I love to read and am currently in two book clubs, so I knew this would be an app I enjoyed creating. I look forward to continuing to work on it in the months ahead.
